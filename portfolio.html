PORTFOLIO

Here is a two player racing game I made in Scratch
To use the red car, use "w" to move quickly and "a" and "d" to move on the sides.
To use the blue car, use the up arrow to move quickly and the side arrows to move on the sides.
The objective of the game is to get the most laps by crossing the red finish line.
I should've added obstacles to make it more challenging.
